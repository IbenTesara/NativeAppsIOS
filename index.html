Waiata

Waiata, or "Song" in Maori, is a brand new IOS-app made to make the life of song-writers easier. It gives them the ultimate tool to quickly write down small parts of lyrics and saving them for later. Organized by either a tag or a song-title, you'll never lose track of your writings again !

Features

  • Quick and easy saving of lyric-items
  • Easy deletable items
  • Sober and to-the-point navigation
  • Organizing on tags or song-titles
  • Easy to use search-tool with triple search-function.
